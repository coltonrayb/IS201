I really like smoothies

Smoothies are thick, creamy beverages usually blended from puréed fruits, vegetables, juices, yogurt, nuts, seeds, and/or dairy or nondairy milk. The most basic smoothie starts with two essential ingredients — a base and a liquid. From there, you can combine ingredients to your liking.
